Mapping of PT ANTAM Tbk. Mining Area in North Konawe Regency, Southeast Sulawesi

  • URL: Project Link

I have completed a project mapping PT ANTAM Tbk. mining area in North Konawe Regency, Southeast Sulawesi. In this project, I conducted comprehensive geospatial data collection and analysis to produce detailed and accurate maps. This process included aerial imaging, data processing, and field validation to ensure the quality and accuracy of the information.
